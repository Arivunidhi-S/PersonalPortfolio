Risk Based Inspection (RBI)

Risk-Based Inspection (RBI) is a methodology used to prioritize inspection efforts based on the risk of equipment failure — combining the likelihood of failure (LOF) and the consequence of failure (COF).

Instead of inspecting everything equally, RBI helps focus on the most critical equipment.

  1. 🔧 Equipment & Asset Registry Module
    • Central database for all static & dynamic equipment.
    • Asset metadata: tag number, type, location, service fluid, material, age, inspection history.
    • Integration with P&ID or 3D model (if available).
  2. 📊 Risk Assessment Engine
    • Calculates LOF (Likelihood of Failure) and COF (Consequence of Failure).
    • Supports both qualitative and quantitative approaches.
    • Damage mechanisms mapping (corrosion, cracking, erosion, fatigue).
    • Risk matrix visualization (customizable 3×3, 5×5, 7×7).
  3. 🧪 Inspection Planning Module
    • Generates inspection intervals based on risk level.
    • Recommends NDT techniques (UT, RT, MPI, ACFM, drones, ROVs, etc.).
    • Links inspection plans to specific damage mechanisms.
    • Supports auto-scheduling & alerts.
  4. 🧾 Inspection Data Management
    • Upload and store inspection results (UT thickness, reports, images, videos).
    • Graphical corrosion rate tracking & trending.
    • Integration with thickness monitoring systems or handheld devices.
  5. 📈 Risk Reassessment Module
    • Allows re-calculation of risk after each inspection.
    • Flags when new inspection data significantly changes LOF or COF.
    • Supports what-if scenarios (e.g., new corrosion rate or process change).
  6. 🔍 Damage Mechanism Library
    • Built-in knowledge base of common offshore degradation modes:
    • CO₂/H₂S corrosion, Erosion in risers, Chloride SCC, Microbiologically Influenced Corrosion (MIC), Vibration fatigue in piping
    • Each mechanism has associated likelihood and recommended NDT technique.
  7. 📚 Compliance & Reporting Module
    • Auto-generate reports aligned with: API 580 / API 581, DNVGL-RP-F101 (for pipelines), Company-specific standards
    • PDF/Excel export of: Risk summaries, Next inspection plans, Corrosion growth trends
  8. 🔐 User Access & Audit Trail Module
    • Role-based access (Inspector, Engineer, Supervisor, Admin).
    • Audit logs to track changes in risk evaluations or asset data.
    • Digital sign-off and approvals.
