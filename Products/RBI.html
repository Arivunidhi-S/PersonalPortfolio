Risk Based Inspection (RBI)

Risk-Based Inspection (RBI) is a methodology used to prioritize inspection efforts based on the risk of equipment failure ‚Äî combining the likelihood of failure (LOF) and the consequence of failure (COF).

Instead of inspecting everything equally, RBI helps focus on the most critical equipment.
