Global Job Posting

Description

The Global Job Posting Portal will be used by all employees of the Group of companies to post and apply for jobs globally. Employees will log in using their official email IDs only. Authorization will be sent via OTP to their email for valid email domains only. Only HR users have the rights to post a job. Employees will be able to search and apply for jobs through this portal.

Responsibilities

  • UI design was done as per the guidelines provided by the client.
  • Created the pages according to the staff.
  • All pages have support for multilanguage, including Japanese, Spanish, Portuguese, Chinese, and English.
  • The post-job, apply-job, business unit, location, and band details are stored in the database.
  • Created a Windows service for weekly update emails.
  • Reports are generated for HR and employees.

Login

Only employees can log in using their official email IDs. The list of valid email addresses is maintained in the database master table. When an employee signs in through the login page, the system checks the email ID against the master table. If the email ID is valid, an OTP will be sent. When the employee enters the OTP, the system checks if the email ID belongs to HR or another employee. If the email ID is HR, the user is directed to the 'Job Posting Page'; otherwise, they are directed to the 'Job Search Page'.

Job Posting

Only HR can access this page. HR users provide job details, global job roles, and job descriptions.

  • Job Details: Company, business unit, department, location, etc.
  • Global Job Roles: Specialization, stream, and band.
  • Job Description: Role, description & accountabilities, skills, experience, and qualifications.

A job ID is generated automatically when the 'Generate' button is clicked. The posting date and job expiration date are also specified. All details are stored in the database when the 'Submit' button is clicked.

Edit and Delete

HR can view a list of all posted jobs. They can edit or delete jobs from this list.

Search and Apply for Jobs

Both HR and employees can access this page. They can filter jobs by company, business unit, location, and job title. Full job details are displayed when the 'View' button is clicked. If suitable, employees can click the 'Contact Recruiter' button, which redirects to the apply job page. Here, they fill in details such as full name, preferred name, employment type, current company, etc. They must upload their resume and click 'Contact Recruiter'. These details are then sent to the email of the person who posted the job. Expired jobs are not shown.

Weekly Update Emails

On the login page, there are two checkboxes: one for the privacy policy and another to sign up for weekly updates. A Windows service program sends weekly job update emails to those who have opted in.

Reports

HR can access all reports; employees can access only the list of posted jobs.

Admin Access

On the job application page, a shortcut key is available. Only one person (the admin) can access and modify the master table data using this page.
