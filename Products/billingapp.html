Billing App (Pattiyal) â€“ Modules & Features

  1. Company Module
    Purpose: Core configuration for the business entity using the POS.
    • Business profile: name, contact, logo, and address.
    • Company-wide settings: default currency, tax rates, invoice formats.
    • Support for multiple locations/branches under one company.
    • Admin-only access controlled using ASP.NET Identity Roles.
  2. Staff & User Module
    Purpose: Manage login accounts and internal users such as cashiers, supervisors, or managers.
    • Staff registration with name, role, assigned branch.
    • Login/logout using ASP.NET Identity.
    • Role-based access control (RBAC) to restrict pages and actions.
    • Staff clock-in/clock-out system (optional).
  3. Product Module
    Purpose: Manage the store's product catalog.
    • Add/edit/delete products with fields like name, SKU, category, price, barcode.
    • Supports multiple pricing levels (e.g., wholesale, retail).
    • Optional: product variants like size or color.
    • Optional: image upload for product preview.
    • Search/autocomplete in POS interface.
  4. Stock Module
    Purpose: Tracks quantity of each product and all stock movements.
    • Initial stock setup per product.
    • Stock-in and stock-out functionality.
    • Automatic stock deduction when a sale is made.
    • Stock adjustment screen (e.g., for inventory counts or losses).
    • Low stock alerts.
  5. Order Module
    Purpose: Central point for sales and customer transactions.
    • Add products to order (cart style interface).
    • Quantity & discount per line item.
    • Tax calculation based on company settings.
    • Payment methods: cash, card, split payments.
    • Receipt generation (print or PDF).
    • Order status (Paid, Pending, Voided, etc.)
  6. Order Edit Module
    Purpose: Secure process for modifying previously placed orders.
    • Authorized users can edit quantity, product, or payment type.
    • Original order snapshot saved for audit purposes.
    • Recalculates stock by reversing previous movement before re-applying.
    • All changes logged with timestamp and editor identity.
  7. Dashboard Module
    Purpose: Business overview for real-time monitoring and strategic insights.
    • Today's total sales, number of orders, and top products.
    • Sales charts: daily, weekly, or monthly views.
    • Low stock summary.
    • Top-performing staff or branches (optional).
